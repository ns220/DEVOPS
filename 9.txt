Experiment 9: Introduction to Azure DevOps: Overview of Azure DevOps.

Steps to create account and create project:

Step 1: Sign Up for an Azure DevOps Account:

(i) Open Your Web Browser:
Navigate to the Azure DevOps website: https://dev.azure.com

(ii) Sign In or Create a Microsoft Account:
.If you already have a Microsoft account, click “Sign in”.

.If you do not have a Microsoft account, click “Create one!” and follow the
instructions to create a new Microsoft account.

Step 2: Create an Azure DevOps Organization:

(i) click on azure devops organization or search it on search bar

(ii) click on my azure devops organizations

(iii) Click on create a New Organization and click on continue

(iv) Enter a unique name for your organization (e.g., YourCompanyoryournameDevOps or
MyPersonalOrg).

(v) Select a Region: india. (it is automatically shows india)

(vi) Enter the characters you see

(vii) Click “Continue” or “Create

Step 3. Creating an Azure DevOps Project:

1.Configure Your Project:

.Project Name: Enter a descriptive name for your project.

.Description: Optionally, provide a brief description.

.Visibility:
    -> Choose “Private”

.Click “Create
